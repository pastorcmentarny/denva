RULES

  1. It is so easy to say 'You will do it tomorrow'. Re-program yourself to say: "Tomorrow is not a viable option. You do it TODAY!"
  2. Get things done through discipline. Discipline allows you to get things done. Discipline equals freedom, it sounds counterproductive but you have freedom because of discipline. I have freedom because of the discipline to get things done. This must be obsession, because nothing not beats hard work. You must be insane to your craftsmanship.
  3. Visualize good things in the times of struggle.
  4. It is OK to fail. It is not OK to not learn from mistakes. It is better to learn your lesson from others mistake. The best thing you can do is to take a lesson from the past into the future
  5. In your life, you will have turn back moment. You can go forward or you can give up. Keep in mind that the guarantee of quitting is that it will never happen. The only way, the possibility, remains if you never give up. No matter what.
  6. Set your goal and go after it. You can achieve big adventures goals through 1000s little steps using routine (a sequence of actions that you do repeatedly) because excellence comes from repetition. Simply. Get better every day and don't waste your life.
  7. Make today better than yesterday.Eliminate things and interactions that made u sad. Remember, This world is heartless to everything.
  8. Think about your language and how it affects your focus.
  9. When you got problem to tackle use consider using PIOSEE method:
    ● P - problem. It requires you to swiftly identify the problem at hand.
    ● I - information. Here, you need to gather information about the problem that is occurring.
    ● O - options. With the gathered information about the problem, you and your team generate options to solve the problem.
    ● S - select. You need to select an option after efficiently evaluating the alternatives.
    ● E - execute. Options are worthless without swift and effective execution.
    ● E - evaluate. After execution, you and your team evaluate the process, noting places for improvement.
  10. Be humble. Gratitude is really about recognizing goodness outside of ourselves.
